About Us

We love to showcase community members who go above and beyond to keep our community safe and clean! At Community Showcase LLC, we strongly encourage everyone to take part in local outreach activities to help make this world a better place!

Our 'Why'?

  • We want to encourage people to do acts of kindness
  • We want to make the earth a better place for generations to come
  • We want to give recognition to those who work hard for their communities
